Case Study : UPI Online Payment  Fraud

UPI fraud is a common cybercrime in India where scammers trick users into sending money through fake payment requests or misleading links. This type of fraud has increased due to the widespread use of digital payment apps like Google Pay, and  PhonePe, and Paytm.

A typical scam happen  in the following steps. First, the fraudster contacts the victim through a phone call, SMS, or social media, pretending to be a bank official, customer support agent, or buyer. Next, they convince the victim to scan a QR code or approve a "collect request" by saying that money will be received. In reality, approving the request sends money from the victims account. sometimes fake links are also used to steal login details.

College students and new users of digitak payments are often targete because they may not fully understand. how UPI transactions work. The consequences include financial means income loss, stress, and loss of trust in online systems.

This case highlights the importance of awareness and careful use of digital payment platforms.
