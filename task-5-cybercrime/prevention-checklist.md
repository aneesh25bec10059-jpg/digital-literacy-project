Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for better security.
3. Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone.
4. Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links.
5. Verify online offers, job links, and messages before trusting them.
6. Do not share personal information on untrusted websites.
7. Keep your apps and phone updated regularly.

UPI / Financial Safety:
8. Never approve unknown UPI payment requests.
9. Do not scan QR codes to receive money.
10. Always check the receiver’s name before sending money.
