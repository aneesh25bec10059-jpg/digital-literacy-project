Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

DO's:
1. Think before you post.
2. Keep your account privacy settings updated.
3. Be respectful in comments and messages.
4. Verify information before sharing.
5. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

DON'Ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
2. Avoid posting offensive or harmful content.
3. Do not engage in cyberbullying.
4. Do not accept requests from unknown people.
5. Avoid spreading fake news or misinformation.
